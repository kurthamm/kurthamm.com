About

I help organizations repair and elevate customer experience platforms that never reached their full potential. My focus is the space between systems where information flow breaks down and where customers and agents feel the friction. I work at the intersection of Amazon Connect, Salesforce Service Cloud Voice, and modern AI so enterprises can finally deliver the experience they believed they were building in the first place.

After years leading technology teams and designing contact center ecosystems, I have developed a specialty in diagnosing flawed implementations and turning them into stable, intelligent, and insight-driven environments. Instead of forcing clients into full replacements, I take the platforms they already own and connect them to the right data, events, and AI-driven logic. The result is cleaner routing, lower handling time, and an agent experience that finally matches the promise of the tools.

I partner with executives, architects, and operations leaders to clarify what success should look like and then build the architecture and workflows that make it real. I run discovery sessions, map data paths across platforms, design event-driven interactions, and align contact center decisions with measurable outcomes. I also help teams adopt AI safely and effectively by introducing translation, summarization, intent inference, and knowledge workflows that fit their environment rather than overwhelm it.

Through Aetheria Systems I work with clients who want a senior partner who can diagnose issues rapidly, communicate clearly, and deliver practical, grounded solutions. If you are responsible for a customer experience platform that is underperforming and you want to stabilize, modernize, or extend it with AI, I am open for new client engagements.
